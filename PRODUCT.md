# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Developer-users, node operators, and hackathon judges who need to inspect transaction history, verify translation proofs, and monitor the live health of autonomous agents (Relay, Inventory, and Pricing) communicating on the network.

## Product Purpose
Provide a high-contrast, premium dashboard that visualizes the status, communication topology, transaction details, and cryptographic receipts of the Relay agent and its connected nodes, offering a clean playground for testing schema conversions.

## Brand Personality
* **Tone**: Technical, editorial, premium.
* **Three-Word Personality**: Stark, Precise, Impeccable.
* **Emotional Goals**: Clarity, confidence, and transparency.

## Anti-references
* **AI Slop**: No generic neon glows, neon green/blue curves on dark background, or floating gradient cards.
* **SaaS Clichés**: Avoid identical card grids, glowing pill badges, and nested border card containers.
* **Color Overload**: Absolutely no decorative colors; use pure black, white, and subtle tinted grays.

## Design Principles
1. **Stark Contrast & Space**: Use raw black and white values, precise borders, and clear spacing rhythm to build structure without relying on colored boxes or fills.
2. **Transparent Verification**: Expose raw data inputs, parsed translations, and cryptographic receipts with raw monospaced typography to display technical truth.
3. **Typographic Hierarchy**: Let scale, weight, and layout rhythm guide the user's eye instead of heavy visual styling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* High-contrast accessibility (native WCAG AAA contrast ratio compliance).
* Support for clear monospaced readability for transaction logs and code fragments.
